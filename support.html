About the App

Baby Tracker is designed for busy parents and caregivers to easily track and manage a baby’s daily activities and growth.

With the app, you can:

  • Track formula milk intake and feeding patterns
  • Record sleep duration and routines
  • Log diaper changes, including bowel movements
  • Monitor height and weight over time
