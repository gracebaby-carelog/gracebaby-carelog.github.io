[CareLog] Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Personal Information (Optional): Name, email, or contact details if you voluntarily provide them.

1.2 Care Data: Care records, health-related notes, images, and logs that you manually enter in the app.

1.3 Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version, and technical identifiers required for app functionality.

1.4 Advertising & Analytics Data: Limited technical data processed by Google AdMob and Firebase for ad delivery, crash reporting, and app performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to provide and improve the app experience, including:

  • Care logging and reminders
  • App stability and crash diagnostics (via Firebase)
  • Displaying advertisements (via Google AdMob)

3. Third-Party Services

This app integrates with:

  • Firebase: Used for authentication and crash reporting.
  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements.

These services may process technical or advertising-related data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. Local Data Storage (Important)

All personal and care-related data is stored only on your device.

We do not upload or store your personal care records, health data, or photos in the cloud.

5. App Deletion and Data Removal

When you uninstall or delete the app, all locally stored personal data is permanently erased.

Because your data is not stored in the cloud, we cannot access, restore, or retrieve it after deletion.

6. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Google AdMob may collect limited technical data such as:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device type and OS
  • Ad interaction metrics

Your personal care records and images are never shared with Google AdMob.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Stop using the app at any time
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Control ad personalization in system settings

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information.
