About CareLog

CareLog is designed for parents and caregivers to track and manage a baby’s daily activities and growth.

  • Track feeding (milk, formula, water)
  • Record sleep patterns
  • Log diaper changes and bowel movements
  • Monitor height and weight

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

Yes. All your care data is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to servers.

Will my data be deleted if I uninstall the app?

Yes. All data will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

How do subscriptions work?

Subscriptions are managed through your App Store account and renew automatically unless canceled.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your device settings.
