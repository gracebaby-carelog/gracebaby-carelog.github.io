CareLog Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026
Last Updated: January 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Care Data:

All care-related data (such as feeding, sleep, diaper records, height, weight, and images) is stored locally on your device and is not collected by us.

1.2 Device & Technical Data:

We and our third-party services may collect limited technical data such as device model, operating system, app version, and anonymized identifiers.

1.3 Purchase & Subscription Data:

When you make in-app purchases, subscription-related information (such as purchase status and subscription status) may be processed by our subscription service provider.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide app functionality (care logging and reminders)
  • Improve app stability and performance
  • Manage subscriptions and purchases
  • Display advertisements

3. Third-Party Services

This app uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: Used for crash reporting and performance monitoring. May collect anonymized technical data.
  • Google AdMob: Used to display ads. May collect advertising identifiers, device data, and interaction data.
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. May process purchase data and anonymous identifiers.

These services process data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage

All personal care data is stored locally on your device.

We do not upload, store, or share your care records, health data, or images on external servers.

5. Media Permissions

The app may request access to photos or videos only when you explicitly select media while creating a record.

Selected media is used only for display and viewing purposes within the app and is not uploaded or shared.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only while the app remains installed on your device.

7. Data Deletion

When you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

8. Advertising (Google AdMob)

AdMob may process:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device information
  • Ad interaction data

Your personal care data is never shared with advertising services.

You can manage ad personalization in your device settings.

9. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS devices, we request permission to track your activity across apps and websites in accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.

You can allow or deny tracking at any time. This does not affect core functionality.

10. User Rights

You may:

  • Uninstall the app at any time
  • Revoke permissions via device settings
  • Disable personalized ads

11. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
