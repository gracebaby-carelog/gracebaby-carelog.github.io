[CareLog] Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025
Last Updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Device & Technical Data: Device model, OS version, app version, and technical identifiers.

1.2 Advertising Data: Limited technical data processed by Google AdMob for ad delivery.

2. How We Use Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide care logging and reminder features
  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Display advertisements via Google AdMob

3. Third-Party Services

This app uses:

  • Firebase: For authentication, crash reporting, and technical infrastructure.
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements.

These services may collect and process technical data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage

All personal and care-related data is stored locally on your device.

We do not upload or store your care records, health data, or photos in the cloud.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained only while the app is installed on your device.

6. App Deletion

When you uninstall the app, all locally stored personal data is permanently deleted.

Because your data is not stored in the cloud, it cannot be recovered.

7. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Google AdMob may process:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device type and OS
  • Ad interaction data

Your personal care data and images are not shared with Google AdMob.

You can control personalized ads in your device settings.

8. User Rights

You may:

  • Uninstall the app at any time
  • Revoke permissions via system settings
  • Disable personalized ads

9. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their personal data.
