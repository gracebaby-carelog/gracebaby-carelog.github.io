[CareLog] Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Information Collection

1.1 Personal Information: Basic information collected during registration such as name, email, phone number.

1.2 Care Data: Care recipient information, medication records, health data, activity logs that you voluntarily input.

1.3 Device Information: Technical information such as device model, operating system version, app version, and device identifiers.

1.4 Usage Data: Anonymous statistical information including app usage frequency, feature usage, and operation logs.

1.5 Permission Data: With your consent, data related to camera, photo library, notifications, location permissions.

2. Use of Information

2.1 Service Provision: To provide core functions including care logging, data analysis, and reminder notifications.

2.2 Service Improvement: Analyzing usage data to optimize app performance and user experience.

2.3 Security: Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and security threats.

2.4 Customer Service: Processing your inquiries, complaints, and technical support requests.

2.5 Legal Compliance: Fulfilling legal obligations or providing necessary information at government request.

3. Information Sharing

3.1 No Sales: We will never sell your personal information to any third parties.

3.2 Authorized Sharing: Information sharing with third parties only occurs with your explicit consent.

3.3 Service Providers: Collaboration with trusted technical service providers who must comply with strict confidentiality agreements.

3.4 Legal Requirements: Information may be disclosed when required by law or judicial authorities.

3.5 Anonymous Data: Statistical anonymous data may be shared for industry research, containing no personally identifiable information.

4. Data Protection

4.1 Encrypted Storage: Sensitive data is stored and transmitted using AES-256 encryption algorithms.

4.2 Access Control: Strict data access permission management, accessible only to authorized personnel.

4.3 Security Audits: Regular security assessments and vulnerability scans.

4.4 Data Backup: Multiple backup mechanisms to ensure data security and reliability.

4.5 Retention Period: Information is kept only as long as necessary; data will be deleted after account termination.

5. Permissions

5.1 Camera Permission: For taking care photos, scanning medication QR codes, and related functions.

5.2 Photo Library Permission: For selecting and saving care-related images.

5.3 Notification Permission: For sending medication reminders, health alerts, and other important notifications.

5.4 Location Permission: For recording activity locations, emergency location services (optional).

5.5 Storage Permission: For local data storage and backup functions.

All permissions will be clearly prompted on first use, and you can manage these permissions in system settings at any time.

6. Your Rights

6.1 Right to Know: You have the right to understand how we collect and use your personal information.

6.2 Right to Rectification: If you find errors in personal information, you can request corrections.

6.3 Right to Deletion: Under specific circumstances, you can request deletion of your personal information.

6.4 Right to Withdraw: You can withdraw consent for personal information processing, but this doesn't affect prior lawful processing.

6.5 Right to Export: You can request copies of your personal information.

6.6 Right to Complaint: If you disagree with our privacy practices, you can file complaints with relevant regulatory authorities.

7. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover we have inadvertently collected children's information, we will delete it immediately. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us.

8. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy periodically. When updated, we will notify you through the application, with significant changes highlighted more prominently. Please review this policy regularly to understand how we protect your information.
