[CareLog] Terms of Service

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1. Services

CareLog is a care logging tool designed for caregivers, providing:

  • Daily care records (feeding, sleeping,etc.)
  • Height and Weight

This App is not a medical device and does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment advice.

2. Account and Use

This App does not require mandatory registration. If you choose to use Firebase-related features (such as login), you must provide accurate information.

You are responsible for your use of the App on your device and must not abuse, attack, or interfere with its operation.

3. Local Data Storage (Important)

All your personal data, care records, and images are stored locally on your device only.

We do not upload or store your care records, health data, or photos in the cloud.

4. App Deletion and Data Removal

When you uninstall or delete the App, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted.

Since your data is not stored in the cloud, we cannot access or recover it after deletion.

5. Third-Party Services

This App integrates the following third-party services:

  • Firebase: For technical infrastructure, crash reporting, and performance analysis.
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements.

These services may process limited technical data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Google AdMob may process:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device type and operating system
  • Ad interaction metrics

Your care records, health data, and images are not shared with AdMob.

7. User Responsibilities

When using this App, you must not:

  • Upload illegal or harmful content
  • Spread false medical information
